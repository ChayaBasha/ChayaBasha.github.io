About Me

Johanna is a Graduate Student at Regis University where she is currently pursuing a Master's in Software Engineering.

She is actively involved in SIGCAS as well as the CSG-ed movement. Her major area of research for her thesis is computing for the social good and what constitutes software for the social good.

Prior to attending graduate school, Johanna practiced family law in the Boulder area. She holds a Juris Doctorate as well as a Bachelor of Arts in Psycholohy and Pilosophy from the University of Colorado.

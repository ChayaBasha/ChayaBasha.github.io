Call me a millenial if you must, but I love me some avacado toast for breakfast. Its easy to make, healthy and delicious. It is light enough for me to start my day energized and has enough protein to keep me full.

Analytics

5 minutes

$$ Avacados are main driver of cost, but it is substantially chepar to make at home than to order at a restaurant.

Great way to use an avacado while its ripe and before it spoils

Ingredients

To make one serving
  • 1 slice Bread
  • 1/2 TBS Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Avacado sliced
  • pinch of Salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • sprinkle of paprika

Recipe

  1. Lightly toast bread slice and butter it.
  2. Make the Egg: coat a small frying pan with butter. Once butter is melted crack an egg into the pan being careful to not break the egg yolk. While the egg is cooking, sprinkle a bit of salt, pepper and paprika on the egg.Remove the egg from the heat when the egg white is cooked but the yolk is still runny (sunny-side up)
  3. Assemble the Avacado Toast: put egg on buttered toast, carefully add slices of avacado. Sprinkle the avacado with salt, pepper and paprika.
  4. Serve.
